Welcome to Nana's Attic

Samantha Stone:

I’m the woman behind Nana’s Attic—born in New York City, polished by couture, and roughed up just enough by decades of late-night glamour. I never bothered with kids; I raised collections instead—leather jackets that survived studio basements, silk gowns that outlived their scandals, and more cigarettes than I’d ever admit. After a lifetime designing for runways, slipping into dresses meant for trouble, and dancing through nights that blurred into decades, I decided to “retire.” So here I am in Santa Cruz, opening a boutique that smells like old velvet, good perfume, and a little mischief. Elegant? Of course. Grungy? Always. And every piece in my shop carries a bit of my attitude—seasoned, stylish, and still very much a crazy free spirit.
